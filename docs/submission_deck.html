The product

No black box

Every score explains itself.

Click any score and it decomposes into named, checkable components. Held out of training, the model ranks founders 2.5× better than chance — and had to beat a shuffled-label test designed to catch self-deception.

Biggest component? Account tenure. The model largely learned that experienced builders have older accounts — the ablation quantifying that is in the checked-in report.

“If a number can’t explain itself, an investor can’t defend it.”

The metric was rebuilt so founders rank only against controls from the same month, and the published number got smaller. The gate is now mechanical: if shuffled labels beat their limit, evaluation raises LeakageSanityError and exports nothing.

“When the gate fires, nothing ships. The weaker number is the one on this slide.”
